BillParty splits shared expenses for a group — a trip, a dinner, a flatshare — and tells everyone the simplest way to settle up. It works completely offline: no account, no network, no tracking. Your data never leaves your device.

Features:

* Create plans (trip, dinner, flatshare) with the people involved.
* Add expenses and split them three ways:
    - Equally between everyone.
    - By exact amounts — each person pays for what they ordered.
    - By shares — someone counts double, etc.
* See balances at a glance: who is owed and who owes.
* Settle per expense — mark each person's share as paid, in full or partially.
* Edit or delete any expense.
* Share an itemized text summary: expenses, balances and who pays whom.
* Light and dark themes.

Privacy by design:

* Offline-first — works in airplane mode.
* No accounts, no servers, no analytics, no ads.
* All data is stored locally in a private database on your device.

Money is stored as whole units, so balances are always exact — no floating-point drift.
