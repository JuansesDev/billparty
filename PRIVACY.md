# Privacy Policy — BillParty

BillParty is an **offline** application. It does **not** collect, transmit, or
share any personal data.

- **No accounts.** You don't sign up or log in.
- **No network.** The app does not connect to the internet. It has no analytics,
  no ads, no trackers, and no crash reporting.
- **Local storage only.** Plans, people, expenses and payments are stored in a
  private database on your device. This data never leaves your device.
- **Sharing is manual.** When you tap *Share*, a plain-text summary is handed to
  the app you choose (for example a messaging app). BillParty itself sends
  nothing anywhere.
- **Uninstalling** the app removes all of its data.

Because BillParty has no servers and collects no data, there is nothing for
anyone to see, store, or leak.
